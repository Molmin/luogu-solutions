#  a[i]:=2*a[i-1]-2*a[i-3]+a[i-4];
就这样，题目就解决了

```pascal
var a:array[1..105] of longint;
    i,n:longint;
begin
        readln(n);
        a[1]:=1;
        a[2]:=2;
        a[3]:=4;
        a[4]:=6;//上文已述
        for i:=5 to n do
                a[i]:=2*a[i-1]-2*a[i-3]+a[i-4];//递推
        writeln(a[n]);
end.
```
